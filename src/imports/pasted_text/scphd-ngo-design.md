# Figma AI Master Prompt — Springfield Center for Peace and Humanitarian Development (SCPHD)

## Objective

Design a **world-class, premium, trustworthy, and emotionally engaging NGO website UI/UX** for **Springfield Center for Peace and Humanitarian Development (SCPHD)**.

This is **not** a coding task. Produce a **complete, high-fidelity Figma design system and UI** that can later be implemented in React.

Avoid generic NGO templates, WordPress-style layouts, and repetitive charity website patterns. The design should feel like a globally recognized humanitarian organization while remaining unique, elegant, and memorable.

**Allow Figma AI to intelligently generate the most suitable color palette** based on humanitarian values, trust, hope, professionalism, inclusiveness, and innovation. Do not force predefined colors.
